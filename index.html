One-time setup

Connect to your Notion workspace to store shots permanently across all devices.

How to get your token
  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations
  2. Click + New integration → name it "Espresso Logbook"
  3. Copy the Internal Integration Token (starts with ntn_)
  4. In Notion, open ☕ Espresso Shot Log and 🫘 Bean Database → ··· → Connect to → Espresso Logbook
  5. Paste token below
