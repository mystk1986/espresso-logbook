One-time setup

This app stores your shots directly in your Notion workspace. You'll need a Notion integration token — it takes about 2 minutes to set up.

How to get your token
  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations
  2. Click + New integration
  3. Name it "Espresso Logbook", click Submit
  4. Copy the Internal Integration Token (starts with ntn_)
  5. In Notion, open your ☕ Espresso Shot Log and 🫘 Bean Database pages, click ··· → Connect to → select your integration
  6. Paste the token below
